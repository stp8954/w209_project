Insights

Jason

User?

People moving from one state to another.
Knowing how healthcare costs differ among states can better inform people looking to make a decision on whether to relocate. Ex. A person relocating from Alabama to New York would like to know whether he can expect to spend more or less on healthcare in New York compared to Alabama.

Data?

Time series data from 1997 to 2017 on per capita healthcare expenditure by state provided by BEA.

Task?

Summarize how out of pocket healthcare costs have increased over a 21 year period (1997 - 2017) by state

Subtasks?

  • Identify the state(s) that have the most out of pocket healthcare expenses
  • Identify the states(s) that have the least out of pocket healthcare expenses
  • Compare growth of healthcare expenses over time by state

Live Demo

  • Interactive visualization that lets users select states to compare healthcare costs
  • Horizon chart added for quick overall comparison at a glance

Insights?

  • Overall, healthcare costs have been trending upward in all states
  • 3 most expensive states for healthcare
    • Alaska
    • District of Columbia
    • Massachusetts
  • 3 least expensive states for healthcare
    • Utah
    • Arizona
    • Nevada
